Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making entertainment accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

Nova Studios is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards across our digital properties.

Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines provide a framework for making web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including those with visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

Measures We Take

  • Providing text alternatives for non-text content
  • Ensuring sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Making all functionality available from a keyboard
  • Providing captions and audio descriptions for video content on Nova+
  • Using clear, consistent navigation across our websites
  • Testing with assistive technologies including screen readers
  • Training our development team on accessibility best practices

Nova+ Accessibility Features

  • Closed captions available on all content
  • Audio descriptions for select titles (expanding library)
  • Adjustable subtitle size, font, and background
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast mode

Theme Park Accessibility

  • Wheelchair-accessible pathways and facilities throughout all parks
  • Adapted ride vehicles on select attractions
  • Sensory-friendly hours and quiet zones
  • Sign language interpretation available by request
  • Service animals welcome in all areas
  • Accessibility guides available at Guest Services

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas of our website may not yet be fully optimized. We are actively working to identify and address these issues. Known limitations include:

  • Some older blog posts may contain images without complete alternative text
  • Certain interactive elements may have limited keyboard support
  • Some PDF documents may not be fully accessible

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Nova Studios' digital properties. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: accessibility@novastudios.com
