USAGE GUIDELINES
----------------
- Do not alter logo colors, proportions, or letter spacing.
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter O on all sides.
- Use the on-dark variant on navy (#0a1628) or darker fields only.
- Use the on-light variant on white or light neutral backgrounds.
- Do not stretch, rotate, add effects, or place the mark on busy imagery.
- Minimum display width: 120px for digital, 1 inch for print.
