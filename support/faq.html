Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Nova Studios and our services.

Nova+ Streaming

How much does Nova+ cost? +

Nova+ offers three plans: Basic ($7.99/month), Standard ($12.99/month), and Premium ($19.99/month). All plans include a 30-day free trial. You can cancel anytime without penalty.

What devices support Nova+? +

Nova+ is available on a wide range of devices:

  • iOS and Android smartphones and tablets
  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, Vizio)
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Apple TV, Fire TV, Chromecast)
  • Web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Gaming consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch)
Can I download content for offline viewing? +

Yes, offline downloads are available on Standard and Premium plans. You can download up to 25 titles at a time on iOS and Android devices. Downloads expire after 30 days or 48 hours after you start watching, whichever comes first.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

You can cancel your Nova+ subscription at any time through your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees apply.

Theme Parks

How do I buy tickets? +

Tickets can be purchased online through our website or at park entrance gates. We recommend buying online in advance for the best prices and to guarantee entry on busy days.

Are annual passes available? +

Yes, we offer several annual pass options:

  • Silver Pass — Access to your home park, blackout dates apply ($499/year)
  • Gold Pass — Access to all domestic parks, limited blackout dates ($799/year)
  • Platinum Pass — Unlimited access to all parks worldwide, no blackouts ($1,299/year)
What accessibility accommodations are available? +

All Nova parks are fully accessible with wheelchair-accessible pathways, adapted rides, sensory-friendly hours, sign language interpretation, and assistance animals welcome. Contact Guest Services at any park for personalized assistance.

Merchandise

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused merchandise in original packaging. Sale items and personalized products are final sale. Returns can be initiated through your account or by contacting customer support.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping in the US and Canada.
