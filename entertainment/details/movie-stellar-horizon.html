Stellar Horizon

2024 PG-13 2h 28m Sci-Fi, Adventure, Drama
★ 9.2 /10

When Earth's resources begin to fail, Commander Aria Chen leads humanity's first interstellar mission to find a new home among the stars. But the cosmos holds secrets that will challenge everything she believes about sacrifice, duty, and what it means to be human.

Directed by Marcus Vale and featuring groundbreaking visual effects powered by NovaEngine 5.0, Stellar Horizon is a cinematic experience that critics are calling "the defining sci-fi epic of the decade."
