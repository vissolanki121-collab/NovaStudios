Our Commitment to the Planet

Entertainment should inspire wonder — not cost the earth. We're building a sustainable future for storytelling.

Sustainability Goals

Our roadmap to carbon-neutral operations by 2030.

🌱

Carbon Neutral by 2030

We've reduced our carbon footprint by 40% since 2018 and are on track to reach full carbon neutrality across all operations by 2030.

⚡

100% Renewable Energy

All twelve of our theme parks now run on 100% renewable energy. Our studios and offices will follow by 2027.

♻️

Zero Waste Productions

Our film and TV productions divert 90% of waste from landfills through recycling, composting, and material reuse programs.

Nova Green Productions

Nova Green Productions

Every Nova production follows our Green Production Guide, which sets standards for energy use, waste reduction, catering sustainability, and transportation logistics on set.

Ocean Conservation Partnership

Inspired by our documentary "Ocean Depths," we've partnered with marine conservation organizations to protect coral reefs and reduce ocean plastic pollution in five key regions.
