Our History

From a twelve-person animation studio to a global entertainment empire — three decades of storytelling magic.

Three Decades of Innovation

Every great story has a beginning. Here's ours.

1992

The Beginning

Elena Vasquez founds Nova Studios in a converted warehouse in San Francisco with a team of twelve animators and a dream to revolutionize animated storytelling.

1995

The Dreaming Tree

Our first animated short film, "The Dreaming Tree," wins the jury prize at Sundance and attracts attention from major distributors worldwide.

1998

First Feature Film

"Luminara" becomes our first feature-length animated film, grossing $340 million worldwide and earning an Academy Award nomination for Best Animated Feature.

2002

Going Live-Action

Nova Studios expands into live-action filmmaking with "The Cartographer," a critically acclaimed adventure film starring emerging talent that launches a franchise.

2006

Television Division

The launch of Nova Television brings our storytelling to the small screen. "Chronicles of Ashenmoor" becomes one of the most-watched fantasy series in history.

2010

First Theme Park

Stardust Kingdom, our first theme park, opens in Orlando, Florida. The park attracts 15 million visitors in its first year and redefines the theme park experience.

2014

Global Expansion

Nova Studios opens offices in London, Tokyo, and Sydney. Theme parks follow in Paris and Shanghai, bringing our worlds to international audiences.

2018

Nova+ Launch

Our streaming platform Nova+ launches with a library of over 500 titles. Within six months, we surpass 20 million subscribers worldwide.

2022

200th Award

Nova Studios wins its 200th major industry award. "Stellar Horizon" begins production as our most ambitious project to date, with a budget exceeding $250 million.

2024

Today & Beyond

With 45,000 employees across six continents, 80 million streaming subscribers, and twelve theme parks, Nova Studios continues to push the boundaries of what entertainment can be.

The Best Is Yet to Come

As we look to the future, we're investing in virtual reality, AI-assisted storytelling, and sustainable entertainment to create experiences that haven't been imagined yet.
