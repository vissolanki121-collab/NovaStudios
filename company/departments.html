Our Departments

Each division at Nova Studios plays a vital role in bringing stories to life across every medium.

Film Production

Our film division produces 8-12 feature films annually, ranging from big-budget spectacles to intimate character studies. We operate production facilities in Los Angeles, London, and Auckland.

3,200 employees

Animation Studios

Nova Animation is home to some of the world's most talented animators and storytellers. Our proprietary NovaEngine technology enables unprecedented visual quality and creative expression.

2,800 employees

Television & Streaming

Our television division produces premium series for broadcast, cable, and our Nova+ streaming platform. We currently have 40+ active series in production across multiple genres.

4,100 employees

Theme Parks & Experiences

Twelve immersive theme parks across six continents bring Nova worlds to life. Our parks combine cutting-edge technology with masterful storytelling to create unforgettable experiences.

18,000 employees

Technology & Engineering

From streaming infrastructure to VFX rendering to AR/VR experiences, our tech division builds the tools that power everything we create. Home of the NovaEngine and Nova+ platform.

5,500 employees

Consumer Products

Our consumer products division manages licensing, merchandise, and retail operations for all Nova brands. From collectible figures to fashion lines, we bring the magic home.

3,800 employees

Marketing & Communications

Global campaigns that build anticipation and drive audience engagement across all our brands and properties. Our team manages media relations, social media, and brand partnerships.

2,100 employees

Music & Audio

Nova Music creates original soundtracks, manages our record label, and operates state-of-the-art recording studios. Our composers have won 15 Grammy Awards and 8 Academy Awards for music.

1,200 employees

Corporate & Legal

Our corporate team manages finance, legal, human resources, and corporate governance across all regions. They ensure Nova Studios operates ethically and sustainably worldwide.

4,300 employees
