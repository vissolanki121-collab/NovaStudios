Our Leadership

The visionary leaders guiding Nova Studios into the future of entertainment.

Executive Team

Meet the people shaping our strategy, culture, and creative vision.

Elena Vasquez

Elena Vasquez

Founder & Chairman

Elena founded Nova Studios in 1992 and has led its transformation from a boutique animation studio into a global entertainment powerhouse. A three-time Academy Award winner, she continues to champion innovation and inclusive storytelling.

Marcus Chen

Marcus Chen

Chief Executive Officer

Marcus joined Nova Studios in 2005 and has served as CEO since 2018. Under his leadership, revenue has doubled and Nova+ has become the fastest-growing streaming platform globally.

Amara Okafor

Amara Okafor

Chief Creative Officer

Amara oversees all creative output across film, television, and animation. Her vision for culturally rich, globally resonant storytelling has produced some of Nova's most beloved franchises including "The Luminara Saga."

David Park

David Park

Chief Financial Officer

David manages Nova's global financial operations, investor relations, and strategic acquisitions. He brings 20 years of experience from leading entertainment and technology companies.

Sofia Reyes

Sofia Reyes

President, Theme Parks & Experiences

Sofia leads the design and operation of all twelve Nova theme parks worldwide. She pioneered the integration of AR and VR technologies into live park experiences.

James Wright

James Wright

Chief Technology Officer

James leads our technology division, including the proprietary rendering engine NovaEngine, streaming infrastructure, and AI research lab. He holds 47 patents in visual effects technology and previously led R&D at a major tech company before joining Nova in 2012.
