About Nova Studios

For over three decades, we've been pushing the boundaries of entertainment — creating stories that inspire, connect, and transform the way people experience the world.

Our Story

Founded in 1992 by visionary filmmaker Elena Vasquez, Nova Studios began as a small animation studio in San Francisco with a team of just twelve passionate storytellers. What started as a dream to create animation that could rival live-action filmmaking has grown into one of the world's most influential entertainment companies.

Today, Nova Studios spans six continents with operations in film production, television, animation, theme parks, streaming, and consumer products. Our commitment to innovation and storytelling excellence has earned us over 200 industry awards and the loyalty of billions of fans worldwide.

From our first animated short, "The Dreaming Tree," to our latest blockbuster "Stellar Horizon," every project we undertake carries the same founding principle: stories have the power to change the world.

"We don't just tell stories — we build worlds that people can live in, dream in, and grow in."
— Elena Vasquez, Founder & Chairman

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we create.

💡

Innovation First

We pioneer new technologies and storytelling techniques to create experiences that have never existed before. Our R&D division has filed over 500 patents in visual effects, audio, and interactive media.

🌍

Global Perspectives

Our stories draw from cultures around the world. We believe diverse perspectives create richer, more authentic entertainment that resonates with audiences everywhere.

🤝

Community Impact

Through our Nova Foundation, we invest in education, environmental sustainability, and creative arts programs in communities where we live and work.

⭐

Excellence Always

Every frame of animation, every park attraction, every streaming experience meets the highest standards. We never compromise on quality.

♻️

Sustainability

We're committed to carbon-neutral operations by 2030. Our productions follow strict environmental guidelines and our parks use 100% renewable energy.

❤️

Audience First

Every decision starts with our audience. We listen, learn, and create experiences that bring genuine joy and wonder to people of all ages.
